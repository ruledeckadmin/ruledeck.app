How it works

Three steps.
No developers required.

🗂
STEP 01

Build your rule table

Open Ruledeck in any browser. Add your input columns — a credit score, a country code, an order value. Add rows for each outcome. Point and click. No formulas, no code.

🚀
STEP 02

Publish with one click

Press Publish and Ruledeck creates a live API endpoint your existing systems can call instantly. Your IT team connects it once — after that, business users own the rules.

✏️
STEP 03

Change rules yourself

When a regulation changes or a new product launches, you edit the table, test it in the built-in testbench, and publish again. No change requests. Changes take effect in seconds.

Unlike SaaS decision platforms, Ruledeck runs entirely within your own environment. No decision data, customer information, or business logic is ever sent to an external server.
