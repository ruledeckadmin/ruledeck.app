3. How we use your data

We use your personal data solely to:

  • Deliver your licence key and software access following purchase

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making. We do not sell your data to any third party.

The legal basis for processing under GDPR is: contract performance (delivering the software you purchased) and legitimate interests (responding to support requests).

5. Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above:

  • Purchase and order records — 7 years, as required by Estonian accounting law

  • Licence records — for the lifetime of the licence to enable support and refund processing

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact hello@ruledeck.app. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate (aki.ee) if you believe your rights have been infringed.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. Support communications are handled via Zoho Mail with appropriate access controls. Payment data is handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy and is not stored by us.

No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Cookies

The ruledeck.app website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not set any first-party cookies and do not use analytics tracking.

GitHub Pages may set cookies in connection with their hosting infrastructure. Refer to the GitHub Privacy Statement for details.

The Lemon Squeezy checkout page, accessed when making a purchase, operates under Lemon Squeezy’s own cookie policy.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated to customers via the email address associated with their purchase.

Continued use of the software following notification of changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
